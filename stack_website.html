Why Stack

Built different, from the ground up.

Stack isn't another fintech rebrand. It's a fundamentally new model — built to benefit both sides of the lending equation simultaneously.

⚡

Algorithm-Driven Matching

Proprietary algorithms auto-diversify Stacker portfolios across matched loan fractions in milliseconds — spreading risk while maximising returns.

🔒

Blockchain-Secured Platform

Born-in-cloud architecture with blockchain and Open Banking integration delivers enterprise-grade security and infinite scalability from day one.

⚖️

Regulatory Sweet Spot

Stack portfolios are not Securities. We align with consumer finance regulators — affordable rates, friendly terms — without expensive SEC compliance overhead.

♻️

Self-Funding Capital Model

Loan capital comes from Stackers, eliminating large investor overhead and default risk. Stack scales sustainably without dependency on external funding lines.

📈

Three Revenue Streams

5% origination fee (upfront), $15/mo subscription (recurring) and 2% management fee (stable) create diversified, predictable revenue from launch.

🚀

Community-Led Growth

Stackers earning >10% returns become the best sales force. Exclusive waitlists and referrals create viral, product-led growth with minimal CAC.
